Cybersecurity in 60 seconds

  • Protect identities: strong passwords + MFA everywhere.
  • Update systems: patching stops most attacks.
  • Watch for phishing: verify links, senders, and urgency.
  • Backups matter: keep at least one offline/immutable copy.
