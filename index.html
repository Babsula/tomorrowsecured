About Tomorrow Secured

Tomorrow Secured is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing cybersecurity education and resources. We aim to empower individuals in Kenya with the knowledge needed to secure their digital lives and contribute to a safer online environment.

Founded by Amar Licina, our mission is to bridge the cybersecurity skills gap, particularly among underserved communities.
